Vegetarian Pizzas

1. Margherita
   - A classic pizza with a fresh tomato sauce base, mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves, drizzled with extra virgin olive oil.

2. Veggie Supreme
   - Loaded with bell peppers, red onions, mushrooms, black olives, and fresh tomatoes, topped with mozzarella and a sprinkle of oregano.

3. Four Cheese Pizza
   - A decadent blend of mozzarella, cheddar, parmesan, and gorgonzola cheeses on a crispy crust with a rich tomato sauce.

4. Mediterranean Delight
   - Featuring a base of tangy tomato sauce, topped with spinach, artichoke hearts, sun-dried tomatoes, feta cheese, and Kalamata olives.

5. Pesto Veggie
   - A basil pesto base topped with roasted red peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and a sprinkle of parmesan.
